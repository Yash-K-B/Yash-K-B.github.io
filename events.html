Mr. and Miss Technovation

“Success is a series of small wins”

The most awaited, charismatic, Mr. and Miss. Technovation is back with a bang. An online event where you will be asked to send a picture of yours And the participant with the highest number of likes will qualify to the next round followed by some other interesting rounds.

The difference between ordinary and extraordinary is that little “extra”. Go that extra mile, Make it happen . Cause no one is you and that is your superpower

Rules and Regulations:

  1. The contestants need to send their photos along with their full name & college name to mrandmisstechnovation2020@gmail.com
  2. Photo size should be less than 10 MB and it must not have any filter effects or edits.
  3. Selfies are not allowed in the competition
  4. Use of auto liker and auto commenter is not allowed. Participants using such means will be disqualified.
  5. Photo should be decent where the participant should be properly dressed.
  6. Only a single picture without any collage shall be taken into consideration. N. B. – If any of the rules is not followed by any of the contestants they will be disqualified.

Procedure:

  • The contestants will send their photo on the email ID provided above.
  • The pics sent by the contestants will be posted in Mr and Miss Technovation page.
  • The jury shall consider the likes of those persons who like the pic as well as the Mr & Miss Technovation page.
  • Top 16 participants (8 boys and 8 girls inclusively) getting the maximum likes for their pic on the page will be selected for the 2nd round to be conducted at IGIT, Sarang during Technovation 2020.
  • Mr & Miss Fame (the boy and the girl who will be the Highest like holder) will get some bonus point in this competition.
  • In the 2nd round the participants will face the quiz test and self performance (Dancing , singing or Solo Act) and PI.
  • Among all the 16 participants, 10 (5 boys and 5 girls) will be selected for the final round.
  • Winner of Mr. Technovation and Miss Technovation will be decided based on the participants' performance in the Final round (Ramp Walk).
  • Do check the page regularly to stay updated with the event and related posts.
